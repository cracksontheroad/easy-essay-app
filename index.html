The guided process

You don't pick the essay type up-front. The app walks you through decoding the brief, choosing a research question, and then recommends the essay type that best fits.

Essay types you can end up writing

Each type is built on the same core skills — Comprehension, Analysis, Synthesis, Argument — but balances them differently. Step 5 will recommend one based on your chosen research question.

Notion Integration

Two-way: push finished essays into your Notion workspace AND pull existing Notion pages in as research sources.

Notion's API does not allow browser-direct calls. Deploy the tiny Cloudflare Worker in cloudflare-worker/notion-proxy.js (3-min walkthrough in docs/CLOUDFLARE-WORKER-DEPLOY.md) and paste its URL here.

Create an internal integration at notion.so/profile/integrations, then share the parent page with the integration. Browser-side Notion calls go through https://api.notion.com — your token is stored locally only.

📚 Set up academic workspace

One-time setup. Creates four databases under your parent page that will last from high school through university and beyond:

  • 📝 Essay Library — every essay, with type / status / level / due-date / grade / citation style
  • 📖 Source Library — RADAR-evaluated sources you can reuse across essays
  • ❓ Research Questions — refined questions you've explored, with SRSAS scores
  • 🗒️ Study Notes — reading notes, lecture notes, idea fragments, quotes

Data & Privacy

Signed in? Your work auto-syncs to your account and follows you across devices.
Not signed in? Everything lives in this browser only. Use Export below to make a portable backup file you can save anywhere (Google Drive, USB stick, email to yourself) and restore later on any device.

Backups include all essays + every step's draft, AI cost log, integrity log, and settings (API keys, Notion config, custom provider). Pure local file — never uploaded to anyone.
